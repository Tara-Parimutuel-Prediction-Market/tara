# The three-tap rule
The entire journey from opening the app to placing a prediction must take three actions or fewer. Open app, see today's top prediction full-screen, tap Yes or No, confirm amount (pre-filled). Done. Under 10 seconds. This is the standard set by Dream11 (220M users), Probo (50M users), and SportPesa (SMS betting in Kenya).

# Market card design
Each prediction appears as a single full-screen card showing: the question in plain English, two large Yes/No buttons, a crowd sentiment bar (e.g., "64% say Yes"), current estimated payout in absolute terms ("Predict $5, win ~$12"), live participant count, and countdown to close. Swipe up for the next market. Modeled on TikTok's For You page.

# Payout display
Primary format: absolute payout in local currency ("Win Nu 460" or "Win $12"). Never show odds, multipliers, or probabilities as the primary display. Crowd sentiment bar as secondary. Always caveat: "Estimated — final payout determined when market closes."
